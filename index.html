Welcome to Naity ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Naity mobile application (the "App"), which provides mock speaking tests. By using the App, you agree to the practices described herein.

Recordings are used solely within the App and are not shared with third parties.

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted their data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
